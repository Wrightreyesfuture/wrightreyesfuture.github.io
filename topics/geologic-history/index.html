Geologic History Principles

Explore the key principles geologists use to interpret Earth's history and determine the sequence of geologic events over time.

Principle of Uniformity

The present is the key to the past. The processes that shape Earth now are the same as those in the past.

Principle of Original Horizontality

Sediments are originally laid down horizontal to the surface.

Principle of Superposition

In undisturbed rock layers, the bottom layer is oldest, with each overlying layer younger than the one underneath.
