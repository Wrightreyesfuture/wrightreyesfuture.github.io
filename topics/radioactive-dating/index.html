Half-Life

Parent Isotope

The original radioactive isotope that decays over time.

Review Flashcards

Half-Life

Click to reveal definition

The time it takes for half of the atoms in a radioactive isotope to decay into a stable form.

Carbon-14 Dating

Click to reveal definition

A method of determining the age of organic materials by measuring the amount of radioactive carbon-14 remaining in the material. Useful for materials less than 50,000 years old.

Potassium-Argon Dating

Click to reveal definition

A dating method that uses the decay of potassium-40 to argon-40. Useful for dating rocks older than 100,000 years.

Uranium-Lead Dating

Click to reveal definition

A dating method that uses the decay of uranium to lead. One of the oldest and most reliable methods, useful for dating rocks billions of years old.
